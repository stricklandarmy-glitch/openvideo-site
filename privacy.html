Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OpenVideo (Apex Clips) handles information when you use the application.

1. Local-first processing

OpenVideo processes videos on your computer. Your source files are not uploaded to our servers because we do not operate a cloud video hosting service.

2. Information we handle

  • Videos and links you import (stored locally on your device)
  • App settings and rendered clip files (local)
  • OAuth tokens when you connect TikTok or YouTube (stored locally on your device)
  • Basic account info from connected platforms (name, channel, account ID) to enable posting

3. How we use information

We use this information only to run the app, render clips, and publish content when you ask us to. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Third-party platforms

When you connect TikTok or YouTube, their privacy policies apply to data they collect. See TikTok and Google/YouTube privacy policies on their websites.

5. Sharing

We share information only when you choose to publish to a connected platform, or when required by law.

6. Your choices

You can disconnect social accounts, delete local app data, or revoke access in your TikTok/YouTube account settings at any time.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect locally stored data, but no system is perfectly secure. Keep your device and accounts protected.

8. Children

OpenVideo is not intended for users under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do.
