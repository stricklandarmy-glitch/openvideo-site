OpenVideo

Turn long videos into short clips

OpenVideo (Apex Clips) helps creators find highlights, burn captions, and publish short-form clips. Processing runs on your machine — your source files stay local.

What it does

  • Import YouTube links or upload videos
  • Auto-detect clip-worthy moments
  • Render vertical clips with captions
  • Optional publish to TikTok and YouTube Shorts
